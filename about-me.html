Maria Sarkisova

Hello, I'm Maria Sarkisova.

In the heart of Georgia, amidst its rich culture and heritage, my journey as a graphic designer began. My designs aren't just about pixels and palettes; they are personal stories shaped by experiences, collaborations, and continuous learning.

Having worked with diverse companies like the vibrant Chain Store Clean House and the innovative teams at ENTER and KTW, each experience has been a brushstroke in my expansive canvas. I also ventured independently, offering freelance services, and cherished the years I wore the hat of a private art teacher, sowing the seeds of creativity in young minds.

While my tools of the trade span from Photoshop to 3DS Max, it's not just about the software. It's about the symphony I create by intertwining them, resulting in visual masterpieces. And, while my design language is universal, I often bridge communication in fluent Russian and Georgian, with a mix of English and a dash of German.

In 2017, Georgia saw me transition from a student of Art and Design to a professional ready to etch her mark. Today, as you delve deeper into my portfolio, you won't just see designs. You'll witness fragments of my life, my passions, and the countless stories I've been a part of.

Come, let's weave a visual tale together.
